About Me

A little bit of who I am

I am currently a Sophomore at Singapore University of Technology and Design under Information Systems Technology and Design Pillar. I was born and grew up in a small coastal city in Vietnam and moved to Singapore a few years back under Singapore's Government Scholarship to do Secondary and Junior College here.

When I was young, I used to dream of becoming an architect. This motivated me to pick up drawing and studying art in order to pursue my dream of becoming an architect. However, something happened during my Junior College year that changed my life: I learned to write my first line of code! Since then, my love for programming grew stronger and stronger until I joined SUTD and decided to take on a programming major.

My passion for art, fortunately, is still carrying on as I often find time to do small sketches of things and people that I observe in my daily life.
