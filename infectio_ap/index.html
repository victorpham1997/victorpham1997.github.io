Infectio

A top-down Run and Gun game that ages a bit too well...
Unity 3D C#

About the Project:
The game is in the form of top down shooter in the run and gun style. You play as a heroic defender of the body's immune system, using various form of medical and bionic weapons ( such as first aid, antibiotic, antiviral, etc) to defeat the bacteria and virus that is invading the body.

Infectio Gameplay Demo on Android device.
The demo showcases different weapons available in game

Game features:

  • Multiple enemy and boss system. Enemy scales accordingly to the game level progress. Each enemy has weakness to specific weapons
  • Multiple weapons available that can be upgraded to become stronger or gain extra ability.
  • Zone machenism that gives player regen skill or extra movement speed.
  • Dynamic camera that makes the gameplay more exciting and satsifying.

    • This project was made for 50.003 Elements of Software Construction course together with 4 more members.
